Mars Weather

Mars is colder than Earth, with average temperatures around -62 degrees Celsius. Temperatures can vary widely depending on the location and time of day, ranging from a maximum of 20 degrees Celsius near the equator during the day to as low as -125 degrees Celsius near the poles at night.
Dust storms often lead to high-speed winds that can shape the Martian landscape and move large amounts of dust around the planet.
